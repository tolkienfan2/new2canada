# Driver's License Information

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Action Required**: You must exchange your foreign license or take a full Canadian driving course/test based on your province's rules. **Check your specific province's website immediately.**

## Provincial & Territorial Licensing Authorities

*   **Ontario**: [ServiceOntario](https://www.ontario.ca/page/exchange-out-province-drivers-licence)
*   **British Columbia**: [ICBC (Insurance Corporation of British Columbia)](https://www.icbc.com/driver-licensing)
*   **Quebec**: [SAAQ (Société de l'assurance automobile du Québec)](https://saaq.gouv.qc.ca/en/drivers-licences)
*   **Alberta**: [Registry Agents (Government of Alberta)](https://www.alberta.ca/exchange-non-alberta-drivers-licence.aspx)
*   **Manitoba**: [MPI (Manitoba Public Insurance)](https://www.mpi.mb.ca/Pages/new-to-manitoba.aspx)
*   **Saskatchewan**: [SGI (Saskatchewan Government Insurance)](https://www.sgi.sk.ca/new-residents)
*   **Nova Scotia**: [Registry of Motor Vehicles](https://novascotia.ca/sns/rmv/licence/)
*   **New Brunswick**: [Service New Brunswick](https://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/services/services_renderer.20056.Driver_Licence_-_Exchange_Foreign_Licence.html)
*   **Prince Edward Island**: [Access PEI](https://www.princeedwardisland.ca/en/topic/drivers-licence-and-photo-id)
*   **Newfoundland & Labrador**: [Motor Registration Division](https://www.gov.nl.ca/motorregistration/drivers/drivers-licence/)
*   **Yukon**: [Motor Vehicles](https://yukon.ca/en/driving-and-transportation/driver-licensing)
*   **Northwest Territories**: [Driver and Vehicle Services](https://www.idtm.gov.nt.ca/)
*   **Nunavut**: [Motor Vehicles Division](https://www.gov.nu.ca/edt/programs-services/motor-vehicles)
