# Winter Tire Laws

| Jurisdiction | Winter Tire Laws |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Ontario (ON)** | Not Mandatory (Recommended; Insurance discount available) |
| **Quebec (QC)** | **Mandatory (Dec 1 to Mar 15)** |
| **British Columbia (BC)** | **Mandatory on specific highways (Oct 1 to Apr 30)** |
| **Alberta (AB)** | Not Mandatory (Required only in National Parks like Banff/Jasper, Nov 1-Mar 31) |
| **Manitoba (MB)** | Not Mandatory (Financing program available) |
| **Saskatchewan (SK)** | Not Mandatory |
| **Nova Scotia (NS)** | Not Mandatory |
| **New Brunswick (NB)** | Not Mandatory |
| **Newfoundland & Labrador (NL)** | **Mandatory (when snow/ice is on the road; Studded allowed Nov 1-May 31)** |
| **Prince Edward Island (PEI)** | Not Mandatory |
| **Yukon (YT)** | Not Mandatory (Essential due to conditions) |
| **Northwest Territories (NT)** | Not Mandatory (Essential due to conditions) |
| **Nunavut (NU)** | Not Mandatory (Essential due to conditions) |

> [!NOTE]
> **General Rule**: Switch to winter tires when the temperature consistently drops below **7°C (44°F)**.

> [!TIP]
> For jurisdictions where it is "Not Mandatory", it is **Highly Recommended for safety**. Check for insurance discounts and local studded tire rules.
